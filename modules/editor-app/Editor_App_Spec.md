# Editor_App_Spec.md

## Scope
Primary score correction and editing experience for waveform, piano-roll, and notation workflows.

## Responsibilities
- High-performance editing (<50ms target interactions).
- Add/delete/move/stretch note operations.
- Quantize, split/merge, ties/tuplets, key/time/tempo edits.
- Playback synchronization for audio + MIDI.
- Revisioning UX (autosave, undo/redo, restore).

## Quality Gates
- Editing interactions measured and benchmarked.
- Confidence overlays and revision controls implemented.
- Export pathways to MusicXML/MIDI/PDF/PNG validated.
